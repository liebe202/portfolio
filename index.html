About

I'm a UX designer and front-end developer with a strong interest in creating experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and visually engaging. I approach design with a user-first mindset, focusing on how people think, behave, and interact with digital products.

My work spans user research, wireframing, prototyping, and interface design, with hands-on experience turning concepts into functional layouts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy solving problems through iteration, testing ideas, and refining details until the experience feels natural and effective.

Whether I'm working independently or collaborating with others, my goal is always to design products that are purposeful, usable, and impactful.

I prioritize clarity and usability in every project. My process involves understanding user needs, defining problems, exploring multiple solutions, and refining designs through feedback and testing.

I work with tools such as Figma for prototyping and user flows, and I build responsive interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JavaFX. I'm continuously expanding my skill set to improve both design and development outcomes.

I'm especially interested in user behavior, accessibility, and interaction design. I enjoy identifying friction points in interfaces and designing solutions that feel intuitive and inclusive.

I'm seeking opportunities where I can continue learning, contribute meaningfully to design-focused teams, and work on projects that balance creativity with real-world problem solving.
